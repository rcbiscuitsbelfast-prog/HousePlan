**Status:** Complete (v3.0) | **Date:** May 2026 | **Evidence:** `research-output/funding/funding.json`

---

## 6.1 Funding philosophy

Fernhill should pursue a **stacked, phased** funding model:

1. **Development grants** to buy evidence (surveys, designs, business plan)  
2. **Capital grants** for fabric and fit-out  
3. **Philanthropic match** to satisfy NLHF match requirements  
4. **Social finance bridge** only if grant drawdown timing requires it  

**Critical principle:** NLHF will not fund 100% of capital. Build the match package **before** NLHF Stage 1 submission.

| Order | Funder | Target | Use |
|------:|--------|--------|-----|
| 1 | **AHF NI Harnessing Heritage PDG** | £15k–£20k | Surveys, options, business plan inputs |

| 3 | **HEF NI** | £150k–£400k | Listed repair (when window open) |
| 4 | **NLHF** | £800k–£1.5m | Anchor capital (EOI → Stage 1 → Stage 2) |
| 5 | **PEACEPLUS (via BCC)** | £100k–£300k | Cross-community spaces/programme |
| 6 | **Philanthropy** | £100k–£300k | Pilgrim, Wolfson, Weston, AIF |
| 7 | **Community shares / crowdfunding** | £50k–£100k | Local match |
| 8 | **Charity Bank / UCIT** | £100k–£200k | Bridge if needed |

## 6.3 Programme details (verified references)

### Architectural Heritage Fund (NI)

- **Harnessing Heritage PDG:** up to **£20,000**; rolling monthly assessment; decision ~6–8 weeks  
- Source: https://ahfund.org.uk/grants/northern-ireland/  

### National Lottery Heritage Fund (NI)

- **£290m+ to ~2,000 NI projects since 1994**  
- Project Grants £10k–£250k; Heritage Places £250k–£10m+  
- Source: https://www.heritagefund.org.uk/in-your-area/northern-ireland  

### Historic Environment Fund NI

- DfC programme for listed building repair — confirm live window  
- Source: https://www.communities-ni.gov.uk/topics/historic-environment-funding-grants  

### PEACEPLUS

- Belfast allocation **£15.1m** — cross-community benefit essential  
- Source: https://www.belfastcity.gov.uk/council/equality-and-diversity/peaceplus
