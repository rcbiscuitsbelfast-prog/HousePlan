## 7. Sensitivity analysis

| Scenario | Impact on Year 5 surplus | Result |
|----------|--------------------------|--------|
| Base case | — | £220,024 |
| Weddings −20% | −£28,800 | ~£191,000 — still surplus |
| Weddings −30% | — | ~£176,000 — still surplus |
| Tea room fails (zero revenue, costs retained) | −£154,280 revenue | ~£66,000 — weddings+wakes still carry |
| Utilities +50% | −£9,000 | ~£211,000 |
| Maintenance doubled to £40k | −£20,000 | ~£200,000 |

**Conclusion:** Model is most sensitive to wedding volume and tea room delivery, but retains viability on weddings + wakes alone at maturity under downside wedding scenarios.
