## What you still must commission

These cannot be completed at a desk and funders will expect them before capital awards:

1. **RICS building survey** (~£5k–£15k)  
2. **Measured survey**  
3. **Conservation architect** options appraisal + drawings  
4. **QS cost plan** (replaces parametric £1.6m–£2.9m range)  
5. **BCC + HED pre-application** meetings  
6. **12-week community engagement** (use framework)  
7. **CCNI registration** + CLG incorporation
