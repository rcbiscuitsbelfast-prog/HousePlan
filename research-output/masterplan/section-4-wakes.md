## 4.1 Market scale

Northern Ireland recorded approximately **15,600 deaths in 2023** and **16,700 in 2022** [NISRA]. Greater Belfast (Belfast HSCT) accounts for roughly **one-third** → **~5,000–5,800 deaths per year**.

An estimated **20–30%** of Belfast families now book an **external venue** for funeral teas (post-COVID behaviour shift). That implies **~1,000–1,740 external wake receptions per year** in greater Belfast, with total market value **~£1m–£3.5m annually**.

## 4.5 Cross-community sensitivity

Catholic traditions often include fuller meal service; Protestant traditions more commonly tea and sandwiches. Fernhill’s **neutral, community-owned** positioning supports both with equal dignity.
