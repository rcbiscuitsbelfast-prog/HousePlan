# Fernhill House — Community Engagement Framework

**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** May 2026  
**Status:** Framework ready — formal engagement programme to be executed  

---

## 1. Purpose

Demonstrate to funders and Belfast City Council that Fernhill’s reuse is **community-legitimised**, cross-community where evidenced, and shaped by local priorities — not imposed top-down.

---

## 2. Principles

- Engage early, before designs are fixed  
- Separate **listening** from **selling**  
- Publish what was heard and what changed  
- Include groups often excluded from heritage consultation  
- Maintain neutral, professional tone on contested history  

---

## 3. Stakeholder map

| Group | Interest | Method |
|-------|----------|--------|
| Glencairn / Ballygomartin residents | Daily use, parking, noise | Walk-in + door knock + online |
| West Belfast community organisations | Programming, hire | 1:1 meetings |
| Schools (primary/post-primary) | Learning visits | Teacher workshops |
| Clergy & funeral directors | Wake offer | Confidential briefings |
| Belfast Hills Partnership | Trails, ecology | Technical workshop |
| BCC Parks / Planning / Heritage | Landlord & regulator | Officer working group |
| Local MLAs / councillors | Political support | Briefings with one-pager |
| Heritage societies | Fabric & archives | Specialist session |

---

## 4. Programme (recommended 12 weeks)

| Week | Activity | Output |
|-----:|----------|--------|
| 1–2 | Steering group formation; engagement register opened | Governance minute |
| 3 | Drop-in at local venue + online survey | Raw feedback log |
| 4 | Schools session (pilot workshop) | Learning needs note |
| 5 | Funeral director / clergy roundtable | Wake protocol draft |
| 6 | Design options exhibition (3 boards) | Preference tally |
| 7 | Youth session (16–25) | Programming ideas |
| 8 | Older residents tea-room focus group | Access priorities |
| 9 | BCC officer workshop | Issues log |
| 10 | Draft findings published for comment | Consultation report v0.9 |
| 11–12 | Amend proposal; final report | **Community engagement report** |

---

## 5. Documentation standards

Every event logged with:

- Date, venue, attendees (count + groups)  
- Facilitator  
- Summary of views (for/against/neutral)  
- Actions agreed  

**Template:** Engagement register (spreadsheet) — columns: ID, date, stakeholder, topic, outcome, owner.

---

## 6. Equalities

Collect monitoring data (optional, GDPR-compliant) on:

- Age, gender, community background (where appropriate for PEACEPLUS reporting)  
- Disability access needs  

Produce **Equality Impact Screening** before NLHF Stage 2.
