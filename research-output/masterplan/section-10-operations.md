## 10.1 Operating concept

Fernhill operates as a **multi-stream heritage venue**:

| Stream | Role | Peak load |
|--------|------|-----------|
| Weddings | Primary margin weekends | High staffing |
| Wakes | Weekday anchor | Medium |
| Tea room | Daily footfall | Steady |
| Cinema / programming | Community + margin | Evenings |
| Community hire | Access + income | Variable |

**Design principle:** **Separate user flows** — tea room/public, wakes (west wing), weddings/events (stables/main hall).
