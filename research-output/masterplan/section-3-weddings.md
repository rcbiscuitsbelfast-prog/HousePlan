## 3.1 Market context

Northern Ireland recorded **7,494 marriages in 2023** and **8,564 in 2022** (elevated post-COVID) [NISRA]. The underlying run-rate is approximately **7,500–8,000 per year**. Average total wedding spend in NI aligns with UK norms at roughly **£20,000–£25,000**, with venue hire typically representing **25–35%** of budget (£5,000–£8,750 per event).

Greater Belfast represents a substantial share of NI weddings. An estimated **~15–20%** of Belfast couples choose heritage or country-house formats, supported by strong social-media demand for authentic period settings.
