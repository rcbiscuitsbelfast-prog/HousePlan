## 4. Indicative Procurement Sequence

1. Conservation architect / lead adviser.
2. Surveyor + structural engineer.
3. QS.
4. Planning/heritage/access/landscape consultants.
5. Legal and accountancy support.
6. Operational supplier soft-market testing.
7. Formal procurement / quotes once scope is fixed.
