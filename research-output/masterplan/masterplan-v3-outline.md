## 3) Evidence Model (Required for v3)

Every material claim must be tagged as one of:

- Verified Fact: directly evidenced by a primary source.
- Derived Metric: calculated from verified facts with stated formula.
- Assumption: judgment input for scenario modelling.

Each section must include:

- Inline citation keys (for example, `[S01]`).
- Reference list with URL, publisher, publication date (if known), and accessed date.
- A short "confidence note" summarizing what is solid vs uncertain.
