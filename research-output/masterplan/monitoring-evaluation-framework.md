# Fernhill House — Monitoring & Evaluation Framework

**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** May 2026  
**Aligned with:** NLHF outcomes reporting; CCNI public benefit  

---

## 1. Theory of change (summary)

**Inputs** → surveys, grants, volunteer time, professional team  
**Activities** → restore building, open tea room, deliver events, heritage programming  
**Outputs** → visitors, hires, jobs, learning sessions  
**Outcomes** → heritage saved, community pride, local spend, cross-community use  
**Impact** → sustainable civic asset in west Belfast
