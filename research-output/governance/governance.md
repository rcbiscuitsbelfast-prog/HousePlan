## Recommendation: Hybrid Charitable Company + Trading Subsidiary

The recommended structure is a **Charitable Company Limited by Guarantee (CCNI-registered)** as the primary legal entity, with a **wholly-owned Private Limited Company as a trading subsidiary** for commercial operations. This is the standard model for UK heritage venues.

---

## Northern Ireland Legal Context

- **Governing law:** Charities Act (Northern Ireland) 2008
- **Regulator:** Charity Commission for Northern Ireland (CCNI) — separate from Charity Commission for England & Wales
- **Contact:** charitycommissionni.org.uk | 028 3832 0220
- **Key requirement:** Public benefit under recognised charitable purposes (heritage preservation, community benefit, education — all apply to Fernhill)

## How the Hybrid Works

1. **Charity (CLG)** holds the building, receives all grants and donations, carries out charitable activities
2. **100%-owned trading subsidiary (Private Ltd)** runs all commercial operations — weddings, wakes, tea room, cinema, room hire
3. At year end, the subsidiary **donates all profits to the charity via deed of covenant** — eliminating corporation tax on trading income
4. Standard model used by Clifton House Trust (Belfast), Wilton's Music Hall Trust, and hundreds of UK heritage venues

**Tax advantages of charity:** Gift Aid (25p/£1 donated), 80% business rates relief (~£15k–£30k/yr saving), corporation tax exemption on charitable income, SDLT relief on property acquisition.

## NI Case Study: Clifton House Trust, Belfast
Grade A listed Clifton House (1774), North Queen Street, north Belfast. CCNI-registered charitable company. NLHF-funded restoration across multiple campaigns. Direct governance model for Fernhill.

## Registration Timeline

| Month | Action |
|-------|--------|
| 1 | Form steering group; engage solicitor |
| 2 | Draft Articles of Association; incorporate at Companies House (£50) |
| 3 | Submit CCNI charitable registration; apply to HMRC for Gift Aid |
| 4 | Open charity bank account (CAF Bank, Unity Trust, or Triodos) |
| 6 | CCNI registration received (3–6 month typical timeline) |
| 7 | Incorporate trading subsidiary; execute deed of covenant |
